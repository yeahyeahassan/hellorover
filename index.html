OUR VISION

The UIU Rescue Rover Team, consisting of 8 members from different educational backgrounds, is dedicated to advancing rescue robotics research in Bangladesh. Their mission is to develop high-quality robotic systems and showcase them in esteemed international competitions.

This commitment has led them to take steps and move forward for RoboCup Rescue Robot League 2025

Their consistent excellence underscores their dedication to elevating Bangladesh's presence in the global space robotics arena.

Media Out Storm After URC 2023

UIU Rescue Rover Team achieved significant recognition after securing first place among Asian teams at the University Rover Challenge (URC) 2023 and 2024. Competing against 37 global finalists from 10 countries.

What Medias Said...

The team's dedication and innovation have not only brought pride to their university but also highlighted Bangladesh's growing prominence in the field of space exploration.
